56.Surat Al Waqiya
(Sura-Waqiya Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai cheyanway (96) Aayatain hain)

56.01 Jab Qayamat qaeem hojaye gi.

56.02 Jiss kay waqaye honay mein koi jhoot nahi.

56.03 Woh pust kernay wali aur buland kernay wali hogi.

56.04 Jab kay zameen zalzala kay sath hila di jayegi.

56.05 Aur pahar bilkul reza reza kerdiye jayen gey.

56.06 phir woh misil paragandah ghubaar kay hojayen gey.

56.07 Aur tum teen jamaton mein hojaogey.

56.08 pus dahanay hath walay kaisay achay hain dahanay hath walay.

56.09 Aur bayen hath walay kiya haal hai bayen hath walon ka.

56.10 Aur jo aagey walay hain woh to aagay walay hi hain.

56.11 Woh bilkul nazdeeki haasil kiye hain.

56.12 Nematon wali jannaton mein hain.

56.13 (Boht bara) giroh to aglay logon mein say hoga.

56.14 Aur thoray say pechlay. logon mein say.

56.15 yeh log sonay kay taaron say banay huye takhton per.

56.16 aik doosray kay samnay takiya lagaye bethay hongay.

56.17 Inn kay pass aisay larkay jo humesha(larkay hi) rahen gey aamdoraft keren gey.

56.18 Aabkhoray aur jug ley ker aur aisa jaam ley ker jo behti hui sharab say pur ho.

56.19 jiss say na sir mein dard ho na aqal mein fatoor aaye.

56.20 Aur aisay meway liye huye jo inki pasand kay hon.

56.21 Aur parindon kay gosht joenhein marghoob hon.

56.22 Aur bari bari aankhon wali hoorien.

56.23 Jo chupay huye motiyon ki tarah hain.

56.24 Yeh sila hai unkay aemaal kal.

56.25 Na wahan bakwas sunein gey aur na gunnah ki baat.

56.26 Sirf salam hi salam ki awaz hogi.

56.27 Aur dahanay hath walay kiya hi achay hain dahanay hath walay.

56.28 Woh baghair kanton ki bairiyon.

56.29 Aur teh ba teh keelon.

56.30 Aur lambay lambay sayon.

56.31 Aur behtay huye paaniyon.

56.32 Aur ba-kasrat phalon main.

56.33 Jo na khatam hon na rok liye jayen.

56.34 Aur unchay unchay farshon mein hongay.

56.35 Hum ney unn(Ki biwiyon ko) khaas tor per banaya hai.

56.36 Aur hum ney unhen kanwariyan banadiya hai.

56.37 Mohabbat waliyan aur hum umar hain.

56.38 Dayen hath walon kay liye hain.

56.39 jamm-e-ghafeer aglon main say.

56.40 Aur boht bari jamat hai pichlon main say.

56.41 Aur bayen hath walay kiya hai bayen hath walay.

56.42 Garum hawa aur garum paani mein (hongey).

56.43 Aur siyah dhuyen kay saye mein.

56.44 Jo na thanda hai na farhat buksh.

56.45 Be-shak yeh log iss say pehlay boht nazon mein palay huye thay.

56.46 Aur baray baray gunahon per israr kertay thay.

56.47 Aur kehtay thay kay kiya jab hum mar jayengey aur mithi aur haddi hojayengey to kiya hum phir doobara utha kharay kiye jayeugey.

56.48 Aur kiya humaray aglay baap dada bhi?

56.49 Aap keydijiye kay yaqeenan sab aglay aur pichlay.

56.50 zaroor jama kiye jayegey aik muqarra din kay waqt.

56.51 Phir tum aey gumraho jhutlanay walon!

56.52 Albatta khanay walay ho thoher ka darakht.

56.53 Aur ussi say pet bharnay walay ho.

56.54 Phir uss per garum kholta paani peenay walay ho.

56.55 Phir peenay walay bhi piyasay unton ki tarah.

56.56 Qayamat kay din unki mehmaain yeh hai.

56.57 Hum hi ney tum sab ko peda kiya hai phir tum bawer nahi kertay?

56.58 Acha phir yeh to batlao kay jo mani tum tapkatay ho.

56.59 kiya uss ka (insane) tum banatay ho ya peda kernay walay hum hi hain?

56.60 Hum hi ney tum mein mot ko mutaeyen kerdiya hai.aur iss say haray huye nahi hain.

56.61 Kay tumhari jagah tum jaisay aur peda kerden. aur tumhen naye siray say uss aalam mein peda keren jiss say tum (bilkul) beykhabar ho.

56.62 Tumhen yaqeeni tor per pehli dafa ki pedaaeesh maloom hi hai phir kiyon ibrat hasil nahi kertay.

56.63 Acha phir yeh bhi batlao kay tum jo kuch botay ho.

56.64 Ussay tum hi ugatay ho ya hum uganay walay hain.

56.65 Agar hum chahyen to ussay reza reza ker dalen aur tum heyrat kay sath baten banatay hi reh jao.

56.66 Kay hum per to tawaan hi par gaya.

56.67 Bulkay hum bilkul mehroom hi reh gaye.

56.68 Acha yeh batao kay jiss pani ko tum peetay ho.

56.69 Ussay baadlon say bhi tum hi utartay ho ya hum barsatay hain?

56.70 Agar humari mansha hoto hum issay kerwa zehar kerden phir tum humari shukar guzari kiyon nahi kertay.

56.71 Acha zara yeh bhi batao kay jo aag tum sulgatay ho.

56.72 Iss kay darakht ko tum ney peda kiya hai ya hum uss kay peda kernay walay hain?

56.73 Hum ney issay sabab-e-naseehat aur musafiron kay faeeday ki cheez banaya hai.

56.74 Pus apney boht baray rab kay naam ki tasbeeh kiya kero.

56.75 Pus mein qasam khata hun sitaron kay girnay ki.

56.76 Aur agar tumhen ilm hoto yeh boht bari qasam hai.

56.77 Kay be-shak yeh quran boht bari izzat wala hai.

56.78 Jo aik mehfooz kitab mein darj hai.

56.79 Jissay sirf pak log hi chu saktay hain.

56.80 Yeh rabul aalameen ki taraf say utra hua hai.

56.81 Pus kiya tum aisa baat ko sirsari (aur mamooli) samajh rahe ho.

56.82 Aur apney hissay mein yehi letay ho kay jhutlatey phiro.

56.83 Pus jabkay rooh narkharay tak phonch jaye.

56.84 Aur tum uss waqt aankhon say dekhtay raho.

56.85 Hum uss shaks say banisbat tumharay boht ziyadah qareeb hotay hain lekin tum nahi dekh saktey.

56.86 Pus agar tum kissi kay zair-e-farman nahi.

56.87 Aur iss qol mein sachay hoto (zara) iss rooh ko to lotao.

56.88 Pus jo koi barah-e-elaahi say qareeb kiya hua hoga.

56.89 Ussay to rahat hai aur ghizayen hain aur aaram wali jannat hai.

56.90 Aur jo shaks dahanay walon mein say hai.

56.91 To bhi salamti hai teray liye kay tu dahanay walon mein say hai.

56.92 Lekin agar koi jhut lanay walon gumrahaon mein say hai.

56.93 To kholtay huye garum pani ki mehmaani hai.

56.94 Aur dozakh mein jana hai.

56.95 Yeh khabar sirasir haq aur qaton yaqeeni hai.

56.96 Pus tu apney azeem-ul-shaan perwerdigar ki tasbeeh ker.
