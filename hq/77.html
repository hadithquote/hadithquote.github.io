77.Surat Al Mursilaat
(Sura-Mursilaat Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai pachaas (50) Aayatain hain)

77.01 Dil khush-kun chalti hawaon ki qasam!

77.02 Phir zor say jhonka denay walion ki qasam!

77.03 Phir (abar ko) ubhaar ker peraganda kernay waliyon ki qasam!

77.04 Phir haq-o-batil ko joda joda kerdenay walay.

77.05 Aur wahee lanay walay farishton ki qasam!

77.06 Jo (wahee) ilzaam utarnay ya aggah kerdenay kay liey hoti hai.

77.07 Jis cheez ka tum saywada kiya jata hai wo yaqeenan honay wali hai.

77.08 Pus jab sitary be-nor kerdiey jainge.

77.09 Aur jab aasman tor phor diya jayga.

77.10 Aur jab pahar tukray tukray ker kay ura diya jayga.

77.11 Aur jab rasoolon ko waqat muqarra per laya jayga.

77.12 Kis din kay liey (in sab ko) thehraya gaya hai?

77.13 Faislay kay din kay liey.

77.14 Aur tujhay kiya maloom kay faislay ka din kiya hai?

77.15 Us din jhutlanay walon ki kharabi hai.

77.16 Kiya hum ne aglon ko halak nahi kiya?

77.17 Phir hum inkay baad peechlon ko laey.

77.18 Hum gunehgaron kay sath issi tarah kertay hein.

77.19 Us din jhutlanay walon kay liey wail (afsos) hai.

77.20 Kiya hum ney tumhein haqeer pani say (money say) peda nahi kiya.

77.21 Phir hum ney usay mazboot-o-mehfooz jaga mein rakha.

77.22 Aik muqarra waqat tak.

77.23 Phir hum ne andaza kiya aur hum kiya khoob andaza kernay walay hein.

77.24 Us din takzeeb kernay walon ki kharabi hai.

77.25 Kia hum ne zameen ko sametnay wali nahi banaya?

77.26 Zindon ko bhi aur murdon ko bhi.

77.27 Aur hum ney is mein buland-o-bhar pahar bana diey aur tumhai seraab kernay wala meetha pani pilaya.

77.28 Us din jhoot jannay walon kay liey waaye aur afsos hai.

77.29 Us dozakh ki taraf jao jisay tum jhutlatay rahey thay.

77.30 Chalo teen shakhon waly saaye ki taraf.

77.31 Jo der asal na saaye denay wala hai aur na sholay say bacha sakhta hai.

77.32 Yaqeenan dozakh chingariyan phenkti hai jo misil mehal kay hein.

77.33 Goya kay who zard rassiyaan hain.

77.34 Aaj in jhot jannay walon ki durgat hai.

77.35 Aaj (ka din) wo din hai yeh bol bhi na sakein ge.

77.36 Na unhen maazrat ki ijazat di jaygi.

77.37 Uss din jhutlanay walon ki kharabi hai.

77.38 Yeh hai faislay ka din hum ney tumhein aur aglon ko sab ko jama kerliya hai.

77.39 Pus agar tum mujh say koi chaal chal saktay hoto chal lo.

77.40 Waaye hai uss din jhutlanay walon kay liey.

77.41 Be-shak perhezgar log sayon mein hein aur meethay chashmon mein.

77.42 Aur unn mewon mein jin ki wo khawaish kerein.

77.43 (aey jannatiyon!) Khao piyo mazay say apney kiey huey aamal kay badley.

77.44 Yaqeenan hum neki kernay walon o iss tarah jaza detay hein.

77.45 Us din sacha na jannay walon kay liye wail (afsos) hai.

77.46 (aey jhutlanay walon tum duniya mein) Thora sa khaloo aur faeeda uthalo beshak tum gunehgar ho.

77.47 Us din jhutlanay walon kay liye sakht halakat hai.

77.48 In say jab kaha jata hai kay rukoo kerlo to nahi kertay.

77.49 Us din jhutlanay walon ki tabbahi hai.

77.50 Ab is Quran kay baad kis baat per eman layengay?
