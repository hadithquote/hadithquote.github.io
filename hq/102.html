102.Surat At Takasur
(Sura-Takasur Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai aath (08) Aayatain hain)

102.01 Zeyadti ki chahat nay tumhein ghafil kerdia.

102.02 Yahan tak kay tum qabastan ja phunchy.

102.03 Hergiz nahi tum unqareeb maloom kerlogay.

102.04 Hergiz nahi phir tumhein jald ilm hojaey ga.

102.05 Hergiz nahi ager tum yaqeeni tor per jan lo.

102.06 To be-shak tum jahanum dekhlogay.

102.07 Aur tum ussy yaqeen ki aankh say dekhlogy.

102.08 Phir uss din tum say zarror bil-zaroor nematon ka sawal hoga.
