79.Surat Al Naziaat
(Sura-Naziaat Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai chheyalees (46) Aayatain hain)

79.01 Doob ker sakhti say khenchnay walon ki qasam!

79.02 Band khol ker chura denay walon ki qasam!

79.03 Aur terney phirnay walon ki qasam!

79.04 Phir daor ker age barhnay walon ki qasam!

79.05 Phir kam ki tadbeer kernay walon ki qasam!

79.06 Jis din kanpney wali kanpe gi.

79.07 Us ky bad ik peechy aney wali (peechy peechy) aye gi.

79.08 (buhat se) Dil us din dharakte honge.

79.09 Jinki nigahen nichi hongien.

79.10 kehty hein ke kiya hum pehli ki si halat ki taraf phir laot aye jaein ge.

79.11 Kiya us waqt jab ke hum bosida hadiyaan hojaein ge.

79.12 Kehty hein ke phir to ye laotna nuksandeh hai.

79.13 (maloom hona chaiey) wo to sirf ik (khufnak) dant hai.

79.14 Kay (jis kay zihar hote hi) wo ik dam medan mein jama hojaein ge.

79.15 Kiya Musa (aleh-e-salam)ki khabar tumhein phunchi hai?

79.16 Jabkay unhein unkay rab nay pak medan tuaa mein pukara.

79.17 (kay) Tum firaon kay pass jao unsay sirkashi ikhtyar kerli hai.

79.18 Usay kaho kay kiya to apnai durstagi or islaah chata hai.

79.19 Aur yeh ky mein tujhhy terey Rab ki rah dikhata takay tu (us say) darnay lage.

79.20 Pus usay bari nishani dikhai.

79.21 To us ney jhutlaya aur na fermani ki.

79.22 Phir alag hat ker koshishain kerne laga.

79.23 Phir sab ko jama ker ke ba awaz-e-buland pukaara ke.

79.24 Tum sab ka Rab mein hi hon.

79.25 To (sab say buland o baala) Allah ney bhi usay aakhrat ke aur duneya ke azaab mein giriftar kerliya.

79.26 Be-shak is mein is shaks kay liye ibrat hy jo daray.

79.27 Kiya tumhara peda kerna zadadushwar hy ya aasman ka?

79.28 Is ki bulandi unchi ki phir issay theek thak kerdia.

79.29 Is ski raat ko tareek banaya aur iss kay din ko nikala.

79.30 Aur is kay baad zameen ko (humwaar) bicha diya.

79.31 Is mein say pani aur chara nikala.

79.32 Aur paharon ko (mazboot) ghar diya.

79.33 Yeh sab tumhary aur tumhary janwaron kay fiady kat liey (hein).

79.34 Aur jab wo bari afat (qiyamat) ayegi.

79.35 Jis din kay insan apnay kiey huy kamon ko yad kare ga.

79.36 Aur (her) dekhney waly kay samaney jahanum zahir ki jay gi.

79.37 To jis (shaks) nay sarkashi ki (hogi).

79.38 Aur duneyawi zindagi ko tarjeeh di (hogi).

79.39 (uska) Thikana jahanum hi hai.

79.40 Han jo shaks apnay rab kay samaney khara honay say darta raha hogaaur apnay nafas ko khuwahish say roka hoga.

79.41 To uska thikana jannat hi hai.

79.42 Log aap say qiyamat kay waqay honay ka waqat daryafat kerty hein.

79.43 Aap ko is kay biyan kernay say kiaya talluq?

79.44 Iskay ilm ki inteha to Allah ki janib hai.

79.45 Aap to sirf is say darte rahney walon ko agha kernay walay hein.

79.46 Jis roz ye usay dekh lein gay to aisa malom hoga kay sirf din ka akhiri hisa ya awal hisa hi (duniya mein) rahy hein.
