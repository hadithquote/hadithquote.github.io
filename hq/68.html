68.Surat Al Qalam
(Sura-Qalam Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai bawan (52) Aayatain hain)

68.01Noon qasam hai qalam ki aur uss ki jo kuch kay who (farishtay) likhtay hain.

68.02 Tu apany rab kay fazal say deewana nhai hai.

68.03 Aur be-shak teray liye bey inteha ajar hai.

68.04 Aur be-shak tu both baray (umda) ikhlaq per hai.

68.05 Pus abb tub hi dekhlega aur yeh bhi dekh len gay.

68.06 Kay tum main say kon fitnay mein pera hua hai.

68.07 Be-shak tera rab apni raah say behaknay walon ko khoob jaanta hai aur wohraah yafta logon ko tu bakhoobi jaanta hai.

68.08 Pus to jhutlanay walon kin a maan.

68.09 who to chahtay hain kay tu zara dheela ho to yeh bhi dheelay par jayen.

68.10 Aur tu kissi aisay shaks ka bhi kaha na manna jo ziyada qasmen khanay wala.

68.11 Beywaqar’ qammena aibgo chughalkhor.

68.12 Bhalaee say roknay wala had say barh janay wala gunehgaar.

68.13 Gardan kash phir sath hi beynasab ho.

68.14 Usski sirkashi sirf iss liye hai kay who maal wala aur beton wala hai.

68.15 Jab uss kay samnay humari aayaten parhi jaati hain to keh deta hai kay yeh to aglon kay qissay hain.

68.16 Hum bhi us ski soond (naak) per daagh dengay.

68.17 Be-shak hum ney unhen issi tarah aazmaliya jiss tarah hum ney baagh walon ko aazmaya tha job unho ney qasman khayen kay subha hotay hi iss baagh kay phal utar lengay.

68.18 Aur inshaAllah na kaha.

68.19 Pus iss per teray rab ki janib say aik bala chaaron taraf ghoom gaee aur yeh so hi rahey thay.

68.20 Pus who baagh aisa hogaya jesay kati hui kheti.

68.21 Abb subha hotay hi unho ney aik doosray ko aawazen dea.

68.22 Kay agar tumhen phal utarnay hain to apni kheti per saweray hi saweray chal paro.

68.23 phir yeh sab chupkay chupkay yeh baten kertay huye chalay.

68.24 Kay aaj kay din koi miskeen tumharay pass na aanay paye.

68.25 Aur lapkay huye subha subha gaye (Samajh rahey thay) kay hum qaaboo paagaye.

68.26 job unho ney boogh dekha to kehnay logay yaqeenan hum raasta photo gaee.

68.27 Nahi nahi bulkay humari qismat phoot gaee.

68.28 Inn sab main jo behtar tha uss nay kaha kay mein tumsay na kehta tha kay tum Allah ki pakeezgi q iyon nahi biyan kertay?

68.29 To sab kehnay lagay humara rab pak hai be-shak hum hi zaalim thay.

68.30 Phir who aik doosray ki taraf rukh kerkay aapas mein malamat kernay lagay.

68.31 kehnay lagay haaye afsos! Yaqeenan hum sirkash thay.

68.32 Kiya ajab hai kay humara rab humen iss say behtar badla dey dey hum to abb apnay rab say hi aarzoo rakhtay hain.

68.33 Yun hi aafat aati hai aur aakhirat ki aafat both bari hai kaash! Unhen samajh hoti.

68.34 Perhezgaaron kay liye unkay rab kay pass nematon waali jannaten hain.

68.35 Kiya hum musalmanon ko misil gunehgaaron kay kerdengay.

68.36 Tumhen kiya hogaya kaisay foislay ker rahey ho?

68.37 Kiya tumharay pass koi kitab hai jiss mein tum parhtay ho?

68.38 Kay iss mein tumhari manmaani baten hon?

68.39 Yea tum nay hum say kuch qasman li hain ?jo qzayamat tak baaqi rahen kay tumharay liye who sab hai jot um apni taraf say muqarrar kerlo.

68.40 Inn say poochao to kay inn mein say kon iss baat ka zimeydaar (aur daaweydaar) hai?

68.41 Kiya inn kay koi shareek hain? To chahiye kay apnay apnay shareekor ko ley aayen agar yeh sachey hain.

68.42 Jiss din pindali khol di jayegi aur sajday kay liye bulaye jayengay to (sajdah) na ker saken gay.

68.43 Nigahen neechi hongi aur unn per zillat-o-khhuaari cha rahi hogi halankay yeh sajday kay liye (uss waqt bhi) bulaye jaatay thay jabkay sahih saalim thay.

68.44 Pus mujhay aur iss kalaam ko jhutlanay walay ko chor dey hum unhen iss tarah aahista aahista bhi na hoga.

68.45 Aur mein unhen dheel doonga be-shak meri tadbeer bari mazboot hai.

68.46 Kiya tu inn say koi ujrat chahta hai jiss kay tawaan say yeh dabay jatay hon.

68.47 Ya kiya inn kay pass ilm-e-ghaib hai jissay who likhtay hon.

68.48 Pus tu apnay rab kay hokum ka sabar say (intezar ker) aur machli walay ki tarah na ho jo jab kay uss nay ghum ki holat main dua ki.

68.49 Agar uss kay rab ki nemat na paaleti to yaqeenan who buray haalon main chatiyal medaan mein daal diya jata.

68.50 ussay uss kay rab nay phir nawaza aur ussay nak-kaaron main kerdiya.

68.51 Aur qareeb hai kay kafir apni tez nigahon say aap ko phusla den jab kabhi quran suntay hain aur keh detay hain yeh to zaroor deewana hai.

68.52 Darhaqeeqat yeh (quran) to tamam jahan walon kay liye sarasir naseehat hi hai.
