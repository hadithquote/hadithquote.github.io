51.Surat Zaariyaat
(Sura-Zaariyaat Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai satth (60) Aayatain hain)

51.01 Qasam hai bikherney waliyan ki ura ker.

51.02 Phir uthaney waliyan bojh ko.

51.03 Phir chalney waliyan narmi say .

51.04 Phir kaam ko taqseem kerney waliyan.

51.05 Yaqeen mano kay tum say jo wadey kiye jatay hain (sab) sachay hain.

51.06 Aur be-shak insaf honey walahai.

51.07 Qasam hai raahon walay aasman ki.

51.08 Yaqeenan tum mukhtalif baat mein paray huye ho.

51.09 Iss say wohi baaz rakha jatahai jo pher diya gaya ho.

51.10 Bey savad baten kerney walay gharat kerdiye gayr.

51.11 Jo ghaflat mein hain aur bhoolay huye hain.

51.12 Poochtey hain kay yom-e-jaza kab hoga?

51.13 han yeh woh din hai kay yeh aag per tapaye jayen gey.

51.14 Apni fitna-perdazi ka maza chakho yehi hai jiss ki tum jaldi macha rahey thay.

51.15 Be-shak taqwa walay log bahishton aur chashmon mein hongey.

51.16 unn kay rab ney jo kuch unhen ata farmaya hai ussay ley rahey hongey woh to iss say pehlay hi nekokaar thay.

51.17 Woh raat ko boht kum soya kertay thay.

51.18 Aur waqt-e-sahar istaghfaar kiya kertay thay.

51.19 Aur unkay maal mein mangney walon ka aur sawal say bachney walon ka haq tha.

51.20 Aur yaqeen walon kay liye to zamin mein boht si nishaniyan hain.

51.21 Aur khud tumhari zaat mein bhi to kiya tum dekhtay ho.

51.22 Aur tumhari rozi aur jo tum say wada kiya jata hai sab aasman mein hai.

51.23 Aasman-o-zamin kay perwerdigar ki qasam! kay yeh bilkul barhaq hai aisa hi jaisay kay tum baten kertay ho.

51.24 Kiya tujhay ibrahim(alh-e-salam)kay moazziz mehmanon ki khabar bhi phonchi hai?

51.25 Woh jab unkay han aaye to salam kiya ibrahim ney jawab-e-salam diya(aur kaha yeh to) ajnabi log hain.

51.26 Phir (Chup chaap jaldi jaldi) apney ghar walon ki taraf gaye aur aik farba bachrey (ka gosht) laye.

51.27 Aur issay unkay passrakha aur kaha aap khatay kiyon nahi?

51.28 phir to dil hi dil mein unn say khofzada hogaye. unhon ney kaha aap khof na kijiye aur unhon ney uss (hazrat Ibrahim) ko aik ilm walay larkay ki bisharat di.

51.29 Pus unki biwi aagay barhi aur heyrat mein aaker apney mun per hath maar ker kaha kay mein to burhiya hun aur sath hi banjh.

51.30 Unhon ney kaha han teray perwerdigar ney issi tarah farmaya hai. be-shakwoh hakeem-o-aleem hai.

51.31 (Hazrat Ibrahim alh-e-salam) ney kaha kay Allah kay bhejay juye (farishton!)tumhara kiya maqsad hai?

51.32 Unhon ney jawab diya kay hum gunagar qom ki taraf bhejay gaye hain.

51.33 Takay hum unn per kitti kay kankar barsayen.

51.34 Jo teray rab ki taraf say nishan zada hain unn had say guzar janay walon kay liye.

51.35 Pus jitney emaan walay wahan thay hum ney unhen nikal liya.

51.36 Aur hum ney wahan musalmanon ka sirf aik hi ghar paya.

51.37 Aur wahan hum ney unkay liye jo dardnaak azab ka darr rakhtey hain aik(kamil) alamat chori.

51.38 Musa(alh-e-salam kay qissay) mein (bhi humari taraf say tanbeeh hai) kay hum ney ussay firaon ki taraf khuli daleel dey ker bheja.

51.39 Pus uss ney apney balbootay per mun mora aur kehney laga yeh jadoogar hai ya deewana hai.

51.40 Bil-aakhir hum ney ussay aur uss kay lashkaron ko apney azab mein pakar ker darya mein daal diya woh tha hi malamat kay qabil.

51.41 Iss tarah aadiyon mein bhi (humari taraf say tanbeeh hai) jabkay hum ney unn say khali aandhi bheji.

51.42 Woh jiss jiss cheez per girti thi ussay boseedah hadi ki tarah (choora choora) kerdeti thi.

51.43 Aur samood (kay qissay) mein bhi (ibrat) hai jab unsay kaha gaya kay tum kuch dino tak faeedah uthalo.

51.44 Lekin unhon ney apney rab kay hukum say sirtabi ki jiss per unhen unkay dekhtay dekhtay (tez-o-tund) karakay ney halak kerdiya.

51.45 Pus na woh kharay hosakay aur na badla ley sakay.

51.46 Aur Nooh(alh-e-salam) ki qom ka bhi iss say pehlay (yehi haal ho chuka tha) woh bhi baray nafarman log thay.

51.47 Aasman ko hum ney (apney) hathon say banaya hai aur yaqeenan hum kushadgi kernay walay hain.

51.48 Aur zamin ko hum ney farsh banadiya hai pus hum boht hi achay bichanay walay hain.

51.49 Aur her cheez ko hum ney jora jora peda kiya hai takay tum naseehat hasil kero.

51.50 Pus tum Allah ki taraf dor bhag (yani rujoo) kero yaqeenan main tumhen uss ki taraf say saaf saaf tanbeeh kernay wala hun.

51.51 Aur Allah kay sath kissi aur ko mabood na therao. be-shak mein tumhen uss ki taraf say khula daraney wala hun.

51.52 Issi tarah jo log inn say pehlay guzray hain unkay pass jo bhi rasool aaya unhon ney keh diya ya to yeh jadoogar hai ya deewana hai.

51.53 Kiya yeh iss baat ki aik doosray ko wassiyat kertay gaye hain.

51.54 (Nahi) bulkay yeh sab kay sab sirkash hain to app inn say mun pher len. aap per koi malamat nahi.

51.55 Aur naseehat kertay rahen yaqeenan yeh naseehat emaan walon ko nafa degi

51.56 Mein ney jinnat aur insanon ko mehaz issi liye peda kiya hai kay woh sirf meri ibadat keren.

51.57 Na mein inn say rozi chchta hun na meri yeh chahat hai kay yeh mujhay khilayen.

51.58 Allah taalaa to khud hi sab ka rozi rasan tawanaee wala aur zor awar hai.

51.59 Pus jin logon ney zulm kiya hai unhen bhi unkay sathiyon kay hissay kay nuisil hissa mileyga lehaza woh mujh say jaldi talab na keren.

51.60 Pus kharabi hai munkiron ko unkay uss din ki jiss ka wada diye jatay hain.
