55.Surat Ar Rehmaan
(Sura-Rehmaan Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai atthattar (78) Aayatain hain)

55.01 Rehman ney.

55.02 Quran sikhaya.

55.03 Ussi ney insan ko peda kiya .

55.04 Aur ussay bolna sikhaya.

55.05 Aftab aur mahtab (muqarra) hisab hain.

55.06 Aur sitaray aur darakht dono sajdah kertay hain.

55.07 Ussi ney aasman ko buland kiya aur ussi ney tarazoo rakhi.

55.08 Takay tum tolnay mein tajawuzz na kero.

55.09 Insaf kay sath wazan ko theek rakho aur tol mein kum na do.

55.10 Aur ussi ney makhlooq kay liye zameen bichadi.

55.11 Jiss mein meway hain aur khoshay walay khajoor kay darakht hain.

55.12 Aur bhuss wala anaj hai aur khushboodar phool hain.

55.13 Pus (aey insano aur jinno!) tum apney perwerdigaar ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey.

55.14 Uss ney insan ko bajney wali mitti say peda kiya jo theekri ki tarah thi.

55.15 Aur jinnaat ko aag kay sholay say peda kiya.

55.16 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.17 Woh rab hai dono mashriqon aur dono maghribon ka.

55.18 To(aey jinno aur insano) tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.19 Uss ney do darya jaari kerdiye jo aik doosray say nil jaatay hain.

55.20 Inn dono mein aik aar hai kay uss say barh nahi saktay.

55.21 Pus apney perwerdigaar ki kon kon si nemat ko jhutlao hey.

55.22 Inn dono mein say miti aur mongey baraamad hotay hain.

55.23 Phir tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.24 Aur Allah hi ki (milkiyat mein) hain woh jahaaz jo samandaron mein pahaar ki tarah buland (chal phir rahey) hain.

55.25 Pus (aey insano aur jinno!) tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.26 Zamin per jo hain sab fana honay walay hain.

55.27 Sirf teray rab ki zaat jo azmat aur izzat wali hai baqi reh jayegi.

55.28 Phir tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.29 Sab aasman-o-zamin walay ussi say maantay hain her roz woh aik shaan mein hai.

55.30 Phir tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.31 (Jinno aur insanon kay giroho!) unqareeb hum tumhari taraf poori tarah mutawajja hojayen gey.

55.32 Phir tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhut ao gey?

55.33 Aey giroh-e-jinnaat-o-insan! zamin kay kinaron say bahir nikal janay ki taqat hai to nikal bhago! baghair ghalba aur taqat kay tum nahi nikal saktay.

55.34 Phir apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.35 Tum per aag kay sholay aur dhuwan chora jayega phir tum muqabla na ker sako gey.

55.36 Phir apney rab ki nematon mein say kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.37 Pus jabkay aasman phat ker surkh hojaye jaisay kay surkh chamra.

55.38 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.39 Uss din kissi insan aur kissi jinn say uss kay gunahon ki pursish na ki jayegi.

55.40 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.41 Gunahgaar sirf huliye say hi pehchan liye jayen gey aur unki peshaniyon kay baal aur qadam pakar liye jayen gey.

55.42 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.43 Yeh hai woh jahannum jissay mujrim jhoota jaantay thay.

55.44 Uss kay aur kholtay huye garum paani kay darmiyan chakkar khayen gey.

55.45 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.46 Aur uss shaks kay liye jo apney rab kay samnay khara honay say dara do jannaten hain.

55.47 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.48 (Dono jannaten) boht si tehniyon aur shakhon wali hain.

55.49 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.50 Inn dono (jannaton) mein behtay huye chashmay hain.

55.51 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.52 Inn dono jannaton mein her qisam kay mewon ki do qismen hongi.

55.53 Phir tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.54 Jannati aisay farashon per takkiya lagaye huye hongay jinkay astar dabeez resham kay hongay aur inn dono jannaton kay meway bilkul qareeb hongay.

55.55 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlaogey?

55.56 Wahan (sharmeeli) neechi nigah wali hooren hain jinhen unn say pehlay kissi jinn-o-ins ney hath nahi lagaya.

55.57 Pus apney paalnay walay ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhtlao gey?

55.58 Woh hooren misil yaqoot aur mongay kay hongi.

55.59 Pus tum apney perwerdigaar ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.60 Ehsan ka badla ehsan kay siwa kiya hai.

55.61 Pus apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.62 Aur inn kay siwa do jannaten aur hain.

55.63 Pus tum apney perwerish kernay walay ki kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.64 Jo dono gehri sabz siyahi maeel hain.

55.65 Batao abb apney perwerdigaar ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhtlao gey?

55.66 Inn mein do(josh say) ubalnay walay chashmay hain.

55.67 Phir tum apney rab ki kon konsi nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.68 Inn dono mein meway aur khajoor aur anaar hongay.

55.69 Kiya abb bhi rab ki kissi nemat ki takzeeb tum kerogay?

55.70 Inn mein nek seerat khoobsurat orten hain.

55.71 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.72 (gori rangat ki) hooren jannati khemon mein rehnay waaliyah hain.

55.73 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.74 Unnko hath nahi lagaya kissi insan ya jinn ney iss say qabal.

55.75 Pus apney perwerdigaar ki kon konsi nemat ko jhtlao gey?

55.76 Sabz masnadon aur undah farshon per takkiya lagaye huye hongay.

55.77 Pus tum apney rab ki kiss kiss nemat ko jhutlao gey?

55.78 Teray perwerdigar ka naam ba barkat hai jo izzat-o-jalal wala hai.
