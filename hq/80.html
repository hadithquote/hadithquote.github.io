80.Surat Abas
(Sura-Abas Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai bayalees (42) Aayatain hain)

80.01 Wo turush ro howa aur mon mor lia.

80.02 (sirf isliey) Kay uskay pass ik nabina aya.

80.03 Tujhy kia khabar shayad wo sanwar jata.

80.04 Ya nasehat sunta or usay nesehat fiada phunchati.

80.05 Jo be parwai kerta hai.

80.06 Uski taraf to pori tawajja kerta hai.

80.07 Halankay us kay na sanwarney say tujh per koi ilzam nahi.

80.08 Aur jo shaks tere pas dorta hua ata hai.

80.09 Aur wo dar (bhi) raha hai.

80.10 To us say berukhi bartta hai.

80.11 Yeh theek nahi Quran toh nasehat (ki cheez) hai.

80.12 Jo chahay us say nasihat le.

80.13 (ye to) Purazmat sahifon mein se hai.

80.14 Jo buland-o-bala or pak saf hein.

80.15 Isay likhnay walon kay haton mein hai.

80.16 Jo buzurgh or pakbaz hein.

80.17 Allah ki mar insan per kaisa nashukra hai.

80.18 Usay Allah nay kis cheez say paida kiya.

80.19 Usay ik nutfay say paida kia, phir iski taqdeer (masalay nafas) muqarar ki.

80.20 Phir us kay liey rasta asaan kiya.

80.21 Phir usay moat di aur phir qabar mein dafan kiya.

80.22 Phir jab chahe ga zinda ker dega.

80.23 Hergiz nahi usnay ab tak Allah kay hukum ki baja aawri nahi ki.

80.24 Insan ko chahiye kay apnay khanay ko dekhe.

80.25 Kay hum nay khoob pani barsaya.

80.26 Phir phara (khool dia) zamen ko achi tarah.

80.27 Phir is mein anaj ugaey.

80.28 Or angoor or tarkari.

80.29 Aur zeitoon or khajoor.

80.30 Aur gunjan baghat.

80.31 Aur mewa (aur ghass) chara (bhi ugaya).

80.32 Tumhary istamal-o-faidy kay liey or tumhary chopayon kay liey.

80.33 Pus jab kay kaan behray ker dene wali (qiyamat) ajaey gi.

80.34 Us din admi apnay bhai say.

80.35 Aur apni maa or baap say.

80.36 Aur apni biwi or apni aulad say bhage ga.

80.37 In mein say her ik ko us din aesi fikar (damengeer) hogi jo uskay liey kafi hogi.

80.38 Us din bohat say chehray roshan hongy.

80.39 Jo hansty huy aur hashshash bashshash hongay.

80.40 Aur bohat say chehray usdin ghubar aalod hongay.

80.41 Jis per siyahi chari hui hogi.

80.42 Wo yehi kafir badkirdar log hongay.
