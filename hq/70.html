70.Surat Al Maarijj
(Sura-Maarijj Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai chawalees (44) Aayatain hain)

70.01 Aik sawal kernay walay ney uss azab ka swal kiya jo wazeh honay wala hay.

70.02 Kafiron per jissay koi hatanay wala nahi.

70.03 Uss Allah ki taraf say jo seerhiyon wala hay.

70.04 Jiss ki taraf farishtay aur rooh charhtay hein aik din mein jiss ki miqdaar pachas hazar saal ki hay.

70.05 Pus to achi tarah sabar ker.

70.06 Beshak yeh uss (azab) ko door samajh rahe hein.

70.07 Hum issay qareeb hi dekhatay hein.

70.08 Jiss din aasman misil tel ki talchat kay hojayega.

70.09 Aur pohaar misil rangeen oon kay hojey gay.

70.10 Aur koi dost kissi dost ko na poochay ga.

70.11 (Halankay) aik doosray ko dikha diye jaeyen gay gunehgar uss din kay azab kay badlay fidye mein apney beton ko.

70.12 Apni biwi ko aur apney bhai ko.

70.13 Aur apney kunbay ko jo issay panah deta tha.

70.14 Aur roy e zameen kay sab logon ko dena chahyega takay yeh ussay nijat dilaye.

70.15 Mager hergiz yeh na hoga yaqeenan wo shoal wali (aag) hai.

70.16 Jo mun aur sar ki khal khench lynay wali hay.

70.17 Wo her us shaks ko pukaray gi jo pechay hata aur mun morta hay.

70.18 Aur jama ker kay samnbhal rakhta hay.

70.19 Beshak insan baray kachy dil wala banaya gaya hay.

70.20 Jab is say musibat puhanchti hay to har bara uthta hay.

70.21 aur jab rahat milti hay to bukhul kernay lagta hay.

70.22 Mager wo namazi.

70.23 J apnai namaz per hameshagi kernay walay hein.

70.24 Aur jin kay maalon mein mukarra hisa hay.

70.25 Mangnay walon ka bhi aur sawal say bachnay walon ka bhi.

70.26 Aur jo insaf kay din per yaqeen rakhtay hein.

70.27 Aur jo apney rab kay azab say dertay rehtay hein.

70.28 Beshak un kay rab ka azab bekhof honay ki chez nahi.

70.29 Aur jo log apni sharm gahon ki (haram say)hifazat kertay hein.

70.30 Han unki biwion aur londion kay bary mein jin kay wo malik hein.unhein koi malamat nahi.

70.31 Ab jo koi is kay ilawa(rah)dhonday ga to asay log had say guzar janay walay hongay.

70.32 Aur jo apni amanton ka aur apney qol o qarar ka pass rakhtay hein.

70.33 Aur jo apni gawahion per sidhay aur qaim rehtay hein.

70.34 Aur jo apni namazon ki hifazat kertay hein.

70.35 Yehi log janton mein izat walay hongay.

70.36 Pus kafoiron ko kia hogaya hay kay wo teri taraf dortay atay hein.

70.37 Daein aur baein say giroh kay giroh.

70.38 Kia in mein say her ik ki tawaqa yeh hay kay wo nematon wali janat mein dakhil kia jaey ga?

70.39 (aisa) hergiz nahi ho ga hum ney unhein is(chez) say paida kia hay jiss say wo jantay hein.

70.40 Pus mujhy qasam hay mashrikon aur maghribon kay rab ki (kay)hum yaqeenan qadir hein.

70.41 Is per kay in kay ewaz in say achay log lay ayein aur hum aajiz nahi hein.

70.42 Pus to inhein jagherta khelata chor day yahan ytak kay yeh apney us din say jamilein jis ka un say wada kia jata hay.

70.43 Jis din yeh qabron say dortay hoy niklein gay goya kay wo kisi jaga ki taraf tez tez jarahy hein.

70.44 In ki ankhein jhoki hoi hongi in per zilat charahi hon gi yeh hay wo din jis ka un say wada kia jata tha.
