54.Surat Al Qamar
(Sura-Qamar Makka mai Nazil hui, is mai pachpan (55) Aayatain hain)

54.01 Qayamat qareeb agaee aur chand phat gaya.

54.02 Yeh agar koi mojzza dekhtay hain to mun pher letay hain aur kehdetay hain. kay yeh pehlay say chala aata hua jadoo hai.

54.03 inhon ney jhutlaya aur apni khuwaishon ki pairwi ki aur her kaam theray huye waqt per muqarrar hai.

54.04 yaqeenan inn kay pass woh khabren aa chuki hain jin mein dant dapat (ki naseehat) hai.

54.05 Aur kamil aqal ki baat hai lekin in darawni baaton ney bhi kuch faeedah na diya.

54.06 Pus (aey nabi) tum inn say aeyraaz kero jiss din aik pukarnay wala nagawaar cheez ki taraf pukaray ga.

54.07 yeh jhuki aankhon qabron say iss tarah nikal kharay hongay kay goya woh phela hua taddi dal hai.

54.08 Pukarnay walay ki taraf dortay hongay aur kafir kahengey yeh din to boht sakht hai.

54.09 Inn say pehlay qom-e-nooh ney bhi humaray banday ko jhulaya tha aur deewana batla ker jhirak diya gaya tha.

54.10 Pus uss ney apney rab say dua ki kay mein beybus hun tu meri madad ker.

54.11 Pus hum ney aasman kay darwazon ko zor kay menh say khol diya.

54.12 Aur zamin say chashmon ko jaari kerdiya pus uss kaam kay liye jo muqaddar kiya gaya tha (dono) paani jama hogaye.

54.13 Aur hum ney issay takhton aur keelon wali (kashti) per sawaar kerliya.

54.14 Jo humari aankhon kay samnay chal rahi thi badla uss ki taraf say jiss ka kufur kiya gaya tha.

54.15 Aur be-shak hum ney iss waqaey ko nishani bana ker baqi rakha pus koi hai naseehat hasil kerney wala.

54.16 Batao mera azab aur meri daraney wali baaten kaisi rahen?

54.17 Aur be-shak hum ney quran ko samajhney kay liye aasan kerdiya hai pus kiya koi naseehat hasil kerney wala hai?

54.18 Qom-e-aad ney bhi jhutlaya pus kaisa hua mera azab aur meri daraney wali baten.

54.19 Hum ney unn per tez-o-tund musalsal chalney wali hawa aik peham manhoos din mein bhej di.

54.20 Jo logon ko utha utha ker dey patakhti thi goya kay woh jarr say katey huye khajoor kay taney hain.

54.21 Pus kaisi rahi meri saza aur mera darana?

54.22 Yaqeenan hum ney quran ko naseehat kay liye aasan kerdia hai pus kiya koi hai naseehat hasil kerney wala.

54.23 Qom-e-samood ney daraney walon ko jhutlaya.

54.24 Aur kehney lagay kiya humi mein say aik shaks ki hum farmanbardaari kerney lagen? tab to hum yaqeenan ghalati aur deewangi mein paray huye hongey.

54.25 Kiya humaray sab kay darmiyan sirf issi per wahi utaari gaee nahi bulkay woh jhoota shekhi khor hai.

54.26 Abb sab jaan lengey kal ko kay kon jhoota aur shekhi khor tha?

54.27 Be-shak hum unki aazmaeesh kay liye untni bhejen gey pus (aey saleh) tu inka muntazir reh aur sabar ker.

54.28 Han enhen khabar kerdey kay paani in mein taqseem shuda hai her aik apni baari per hazir hoga.

54.29 Unhon ney apney sathi ko awaz di jiss ney (untni per) waar kiya aur (uss ki) kochen kaat den.

54.30 Pus kiyon ker hua mera azab aur mera darana.

54.31 Hum ney unn per aik cheekh bheji pus aisay hogaye jaisay baar bananay walay ki rondhi hui ghaas.

54.32 Aur hum ney naseehat kay liye quran ko aasan kerdia hai pus kiya hai koi jo naseehat qabool keray.

54.33 Qom-e-Loot ney bhi daraney walon ki takzeeb ki.

54.34 Be-shak hum ney unn per pathar barsaney wali hawa bheji siwaye loot(alh-e-salam) kay ghar walon kay waqt nijat deydi.

54.35 Apney ehsan say her her shukarguzaar ko hum issi tarah badla detay hain.

54.36 Yaqeenan loot (alh-e-salam) ney unhen humari pakar say daraya tha lekinunhon ney daraney walon kay baray mein (shak-o-shuba aur) jhagra kiya.

54.37 Aur inn loot (alh-e-salam) ko unkay mehmanon kay baray mein phuslaya pus hum ney unki aankhen andhi kerden (aur kehdia) mera azab aur mera darana chakho.

54.38 Aur yaqeeni baat hai kay unhen subah saweray hi aik jagah pakarney walay muqarra azab ney ghaarat kerdia.

54.39 Pus meray azab aur meray darawey ka maza chakho.

54.40 Aur yaqeenan hum ney quran ko Pind-o-waaz kay liye aasan kerdia hai pus kiya koi hai naseehat pakarney wala.

54.41 Aur firaoniyon kay pass bhi daraney walay aaye.

54.42 Unhon ney humari tamamnishaniyan jhutlayen pus hum ney unhen baray ghalib qavi pakarney walay ki tarah pakar liya.

54.43 (Aey qureshiyo!) kiya tumharay kafir inn kafiron say kuch behtar hain? ya tumharay liye agli kitabon mein chutkara likha hua hai?

54.44 Ya yeh kehtay hain kay hum ghalba panay wali janat hain.

54.45 Unqareeb yeh jamat shikast di jayegi aur peeth deyker bhagey gi.

54.46 Bulkay qayamat ki ghari unkay waday kay waqt hai aur qayamat bari sakht aur kerwi cheez hai.

54.47 Be-shak gunehgaar gumrahi mein aur azab mein hain.

54.48 Jiss din woh apney mun kay bal aag mein ghaseetay jayen gey.(aur unn say kaha jayega) dozakh ki aag lagney kay mazay chakho.

54.49 Be-shak hum ney her cheez ko aik (muqarra) andazay per peda kia hai.

54.50 Aur humara hukum sirf aik dafa (ka aik kalma) hi hota hai jaisey aankh ka jhapakna.

54.51 Aur hum ney tum jaisey bohteron ko halak kerdia hai pus koi hai naseehat lenay wala.

54.52 Jo kuch enhon ney (aemaal) kiye hain sab naam-e-aemaal mein likhay huye hain.

54.53 (Issi tarah) her choti bari baat bhi likhi hui hai.

54.54 Yaqeenan humara darr rakhney walay jannaton aur neharon mein hongay.

54.55 Raasti aur izzat ki bethak mein qudrat walay badshah kay pass.
